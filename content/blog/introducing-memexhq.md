Your AI doesn't know your business. Every Claude Code session, every ChatGPT conversation, every Copilot suggestion — they all start from zero.

## The Problem

Modern AI tools are incredibly powerful, but they have no institutional memory. Your team has accumulated years of knowledge: architecture decisions, failed experiments, client preferences, and hard-won lessons. None of it is accessible to your AI tools.

The result? Engineers spend 20+ minutes per session re-explaining context. AI confidently suggests approaches you already tried and abandoned. Marketing writes campaigns for features clients explicitly don't want.

## Our Solution

MemexHQ is a context layer that runs on your local network. It connects to every tool your team uses — GitHub, Jira, Slack, meeting recordings, sales calls — and builds a unified knowledge graph of your business.

When anyone on your team queries an AI, MemexHQ automatically enriches the prompt with relevant context. No manual copying. No re-explaining. Your AI finally knows your business.

### Key Features

- **Local-first**: All data stays on your network. Zero cloud egress.
- **Universal**: Works with Claude Code, ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini, and any LLM.
- **Automatic**: Context injection happens seamlessly. No workflow changes required.
- **Permission-aware**: Respects your existing access controls.
